Pharmacy Inventory Management System

Internship Project

A web-based pharmacy inventory management system developed during an internship at AA Apotek Anugrah. The system helps pharmacy staff manage medicine data, record stock in and out transactions, monitor expiration dates, and track inventory efficiently.

  • Multi-Role Access: Separate dashboards for Admin and Owner with role-based system management.
  • Medicine Management: Manage medicine data including name, category, and expiration date.
  • Stock In & Out Tracking: Record incoming and outgoing medicine stock automatically.
  • Sales Transactions: Record medicine purchases and automatically update stock quantities.
  • Expiration Monitoring: Detect medicines that are expired or close to expiration.
  • Search & Filtering: Quickly find medicines using filtering and search features.

University Registration System

A web-based new student admission system developed as part of a Web Developer Certification (LSP). The application allows prospective students to register online, submit registration forms, track application status, and manage payment confirmations, while administrators can verify accounts, review documents, and manage admission processes through an admin dashboard.

  • User Authentication: Registration and login system with account verification handled by the admin.
  • Student Registration Form: Online form for submitting personal data and study program selection.
  • Admin Verification: Admin dashboard to verify user accounts and review applicant data.
  • Selection Management: Admin can approve or reject applicants during the admission process.
  • Payment Confirmation: Students upload proof of payment which is then verified by the admin.
  • Application Status Tracking: Students can track registration progress and admission status through their dashboard.
